Mac and Cheese

Description: This recipe is of a creamy homemade mac and cheese that is bound to have your mouths watering.

Ingredients

  • elbow macaroni
  • olive oil
  • flour
  • milk
  • cheese

Steps

  1. 1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. 2. cook pasta 1 min
  3. 3. melt butter
  4. 4. whisk flour in
  5. 5. add cheese
  6. 6. add macaroni
